Mission Briefing

Welcome to WOLFLIX. Today we watch the trailer for Project Hail Mary. A story about waking up millions of miles from home with no memory of who you are.

Comm Link

Earth Command

Read the simulated text between Ryland and Earth Command.

Ryland: What is my mission? Do I have to fly the ship?
Command: No. The ship flies automatically. You don't have to fly it.
Ryland: Then what do I do?
Command: You must find the alien microbe. Do not fail.

Discussion:

"Don't have to" means it is not necessary. What does he NOT have to do? And what MUST he do?
